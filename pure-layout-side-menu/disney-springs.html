Disney Springs

Intro To Tips

What makes Disney Springs so magical is the close parking garages, the free parking in addition to the free entry. This saves more money to splurge on your experience and yourself. Disney Springs is the shopping and dining district of Walt Disney World, and has undergone a complete rejuvenation with expansions that allow you to make a whole day and then some out of one place. Tom Bricker of Travel+Lesiure said, "Disney Springs is the place to go on a day you're not going to the theme parks." The ambiance and the free entertainment is what makes Disney Springs one of the top places to visit any time of the week at any hour of the day.

One of the interesting things about Disney Springs is the division to different sections, all of which are worth visiting. The Marketplace is where all of the Disney related shops are located like World of Disney, the biggest Disney store equivalent there (that's including the parks). The other side of Disney Springs is where most of the non-Disney shops and dining are located as well as more adult-oriented areas including clubs, theatres, etc. Because there's so much Disney Springs has to offer, there's five things that should be at the top of your to do list when it comes to exploring it.

To add another bonus it's located right in the heart of all of the Disney Spring restaurants and snack shops, so one can enjoy being young at heart while preparing to eat on the many delicacies that are right around the corner.
